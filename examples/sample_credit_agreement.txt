CREDIT AGREEMENT

Section 1.01 Defined Terms
"Applicable Margin" means 2.25% per annum for SOFR loans.
"Maturity Date" means March 31, 2031.
"Leverage Ratio" means total funded debt divided by EBITDA.

Section 2.01 Revolving Commitments
Borrower may request up to $250 million revolving commitments.
The interest rate will be Term SOFR plus Applicable Margin.

Section 6.02 Financial Covenants
Borrower shall maintain a maximum Leverage Ratio of 4.50 to 1.00.
Borrower shall maintain a minimum Interest Coverage Ratio of 3.00 to 1.00.

Section 8.01 Events of Default
Events of Default include payment default, bankruptcy default, and covenant default.
A covenant default occurs when obligations in Section 6.02 are breached.

Section 9.04 References
For interest pricing, see Section 2.01 and Applicable Margin as defined in "Applicable Margin".
